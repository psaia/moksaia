july 26, 2020 // Maryland + South Carolina

I’ve been waiting for our goodbye

We’ll stand on the corner where we first met in san francisco

You’ll look me in the eye

Tell me that we meant something

Tell me we’re worth remembering,

Worth continuing

And i’ll know when we part ways

That i didn’t lose you to time

I didn’t lose you to heartbreak

I didn’t lose you because we got older and we moved on

To other people, places, directions

I tried to stop it

Tried to reach out and pull us back

But i could tell you wanted to go

Who was i to hold you here?

I was the one you left behind

I was the time before this time now

I was the girl you could have loved

Who could have loved you

I don’t think there’s a goodbye big enough

For all we could have been

Words by Karen Mok
